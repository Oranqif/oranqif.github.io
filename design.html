PlayMobileGames ( Figma )

For the C218 module, I had to design a wireframe mobile application with a few of major features included.

I came up with the idea of a mobile gaming application that has all of the mobile games stored into one application.

Furthermore, people can find the game(s) they like using the search and filter function categorized by rating, genre and gameplay.

This is one of my first major step into the world of UI/UX designs and showcases my design principles and creativity.
