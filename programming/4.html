Introduction

A virtual reality game where the player woke up inside a building confused about their surrounding. They found out that they are trapped inside a tower thanks to a bored wizard that wanted to play a game with them. The instruction was simple, find all the clues then solve all three puzzles inside the wizard tower to earn their freedom.

About the project

I was assigned by my lecturer to create a virtual reality game that involved solving puzzles. When thinking up of a theme to base the game around with, what came to mind was this...

  • The genre will be an escape room situated in a cartoon fantasy world.
  • The narrative will have the player awaken inside the wizard tower where he will have to find clues and solve various puzzles to get out.
  • Inspired by the fantastical setting this game will take place in.
...The idea of utilizing magical devices to aid in your escape was too great for me to pass up.

Why I had decided to go with the idea was because it would allow me to not be restricted by realism, give more space for suspension of disbelief from the player playing this game, and more importantly the opportunity to create a more fun puzzle for better gameplay.

Next, for the puzzles. The puzzles I ended up creating were...
  • Finding all three of the apples to put into a cauldron to obtain the crystal for the combination crystal puzzle.
  • Finding all three pieces of a key and to bring them back to a table to reconstruct it for the chest.
  • Solving the combination crystal puzzle using clue from a note to finally escape the tower.
...Despite them not looking like how I had imagined them inside my head, the puzzles were somewhat magical in nature and served the gameplay well enough for the player to engage in. The images below show what these puzzles looked like inside the game...
